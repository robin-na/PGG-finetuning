# GAME RULES
This is an online public goods game (PGG).
Each round, the 20 coins begin in the shared pot. Each player chooses how many coins to withdraw for private use, so the resulting contribution must be either 0 or 20.
Players do not see others' choices before deciding.
The shared pot is multiplied by 2.5 and split equally among all active players.
After contributions are revealed and redistributed, players may punish other players.
Each punishment unit costs 1 coin to the source and deducts 2 coins from the target.
Players may send messages to the group whenever they choose to do so.
Players know that the game lasts 23 rounds.
Players cannot identify who punished them in their summary information.
At the summary stage (`ROUND N SUMMARY SHOWN TO PLAYERS`), each player sees their own net payoff of the round, along with the amount they used for punishing other players and amount deducted from punishment.
The corresponding information of the peers are also shown.
