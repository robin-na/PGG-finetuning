# OSPsychBig5 vs Twin Raw Study Shift

- OpenPsych raw proxy: all US respondents from `IPIP-FFM-data-8Nov2018.zip` (`n=546403`)
- Twin raw proxy: participants with complete Big Five summary features in `twin_extended_profiles.jsonl` (`n=2058`)
- Validation: mean item TVD between raw OpenPsych US marginals and the SimBench US `OSPsychBig5` rows = 0.0284
