# RULES
- This is one standalone game. Treat it independently from any other game.
- There are 3 rounds.
- Each round has 3 turns, one proposal per player, in the same order every round.
- Turn order each round: `dog-orange-384`, `buffalo-red-6989`, `cat-pink-8143`
- On a turn, the sender proposes one trade: request a quantity of one chip color and offer a quantity of a different chip color.
- The sender cannot offer more chips than they currently hold.
- Other active players respond simultaneously and privately with accept or decline.
- If nobody accepts, the trade is declined.
- If one player accepts, that player is the recipient and the trade executes.
- If multiple players accept, one accepting player is chosen at random as the recipient and the trade executes.
- Players see the public trade history and current chip holdings, but each player only knows their own private chip values.
- The behavioral goal is to maximize the total value of chips held by the acting player.
