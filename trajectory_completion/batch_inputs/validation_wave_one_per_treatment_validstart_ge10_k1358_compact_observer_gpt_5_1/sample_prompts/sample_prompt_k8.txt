# GAME RULES
This is an online public goods game (PGG).
Each round, the 20 coins begin in the shared pot. Each player chooses how many coins to withdraw for private use, so the resulting contribution must be either 0 or 20.
Players do not see others' choices before deciding.
The shared pot is multiplied by 2.9 and split equally among all active players.
After contributions are revealed and redistributed, players may reward other players.

Players may send messages to the group whenever they choose to do so.
Players know that the game lasts 26 rounds.
Players cannot identify who rewarded them in their summary information.
At the summary stage (`ROUND N SUMMARY SHOWN TO PLAYERS`), each player sees their own net payoff of the round, along with the amount they used for rewarding other players and amount received for reward.
