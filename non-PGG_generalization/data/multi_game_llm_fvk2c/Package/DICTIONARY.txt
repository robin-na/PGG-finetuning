SubjectID: subject identifier (integer)

PersonalizedTreatment: personalization of AI (dummy 1=personalized, 0 = unpersonalized)
Scenario: role scenario (string: "AISupport" "NoAISupport")

PD: cooperation in Prisoner's Dilemma Game (dummy: 1=cooperation, 0=defection)
SH: cooperation in Stag Hunt Game (dummy: 1=cooperation, 0=no copperation)
C: coordination (dummy: 1=coordination, 0=no coordination)
Proposer_Delegation: delegation decision as proposer (dummy: 1=delegation, 0=no delegation)
Responder_Delegation: delegation decision as responder (dummy: 1=delegation, 0=no delegation)
Receiver_Delegation: delegation decision as receiver (dummy: 1=delegation, 0=no delegation)
Sender_Delegation: delegation decision as sender (dummy: 1=delegation, 0=no delegation)

SH_Delegation: delegation decision in SH (dummy: 1=delegation, 0=no delegation)
C_Delegation: delegation decision in C (dummy: 1=delegation, 0=no delegation)
count_ChangedBrowserWindow: number of times browser window is changed (number)
DurationInSec: duration in seconds to complete experiment (number)
KnowledgeChatGPT: if subject knows CHatGPT (string: "Yes" "No")
UsageChatGPT: times ChatGPT used (string: "Never" "11 - 50 times" "6 - 10 times" "1 - 5 times" "Over 50 times")
IntuitionThoughtfulness: binary preference for 1st or 2nd concept (number: 1 or 2)
IntroversionExtraverison: binary preference for 1st or 2nd concept (number: 1 or 2)
FairnessEfficiency: binary preference for 1st or 2nd concept (number: 1 or 2)
ChaosBoredom: binary preference for 1st or 2nd concept (number: 1 or 2)
SelfishnessAltruism: binary preference for 1st or 2nd concept (number: 1 or 2)
NoveltyReliability: binary preference for 1st or 2nd concept (number: 1 or 2)
TruthHarmony: binary preference for 1st or 2nd concept (number: 1 or 2)
_DifficultyPredictionAI: AI difficult to predict (5-point Likert scale: "Strongly disagree" - "Strongly agree") 
Q_DifficultyPredictionHuman: AI difficult to predict (5-point Likert scale: "Strongly disagree" - "Strongly agree") 
Q_AITrustworthy: AI is trustworthy (5-point Likert scale: "Strongly disagree" - "Strongly agree") 
Q_HumanTrustworthy: humans are trustworthy (5-point Likert scale: "Strongly disagree" - "Strongly agree") 
Q_AIReflectsHuman: AI reflects human preferences (5-point Likert scale: "Strongly disagree" - "Strongly agree") 
Q_DelegationBelief: others' will delegate to AI (5-point Likert scale: "Strongly disagree" - "Strongly agree") 
Q_DelegationAppropriate: delegation to AI appropriate (5-point Likert scale: "Strongly disagree" - "Strongly agree") 
Q_EqualityWithAI: equality concerns AI (5-point Likert scale: "Strongly disagree" - "Strongly agree") 
Q_EqualityWithHuman: equality concerns human(5-point Likert scale: "Strongly disagree" - "Strongly agree") 
Age: age in years (number)
Gender: subjects gender (string: "Man" "Woman" "Non-binary" "Self-description")
Education: highest degree or qualification (string) 
Exclusion_Disturb: written statement 
ExclusionUsageGPT: written statement
personality_string: string of 1s and 2s indicating the subjects' personality
